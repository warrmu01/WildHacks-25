About 'The Watcher'

The Watcher is a productivity-focused Chrome extension built to help users stay on track — with a little bit of personality.

We know how easy it is to drift off into the internet rabbit hole. That’s why The Watcher keeps a close (yet friendly) eye on your browser activity, helping you remain focused in real time.

How It Works

  • Tracks open tabs, time spent, and detects when you're inactive.
  • Uses the Gemini API to check if tabs are relevant to your current task.
  • Integrates with your Google Calendar to understand your schedule.

Tab Classification

  • Good Tabs – Relevant to your task. You’ll get motivating nudges to keep going.
  • Neutral Tabs – May or may not be useful. We’ll keep an eye on it.
  • Bad Tabs – Like Netflix or Instagram. The Watcher will call you out.

The Watcher is your digital accountability ghost — designed and developed by Muhammad Mughees Jatt, Sarab Aftab, Nipun Yonjan, and Abdullah Ali for WildHacks-25.
